school achievements

Inter School Debate On Environment Pollution
Won first prize in inter school debate on environment pollution

Our school won the debate competition. The topic for that was 'Environment Pollution' and We were asked to speak for the topic. This was first time in our school participated in the debate.Our school had spoken very well. It seemed that the audience were listening to us with interest. When the results were annouced our name was announced at the first as we had won the first prize in 15 schools.We had won the first prize in the first ever debate competion attended by the School

Inter School Football Competition
Came second in inter school football competition

Recently, Our School got the opportunity to witness a inter-school football match that was played between Khalsa Higher Secondary School, Qadian and DAV Senior Secondary School, Delhi. The match was played at the playgrounds of SN College. The entire ground was swept clean and the sides decorated. There were chairs on one side and rest of the ground was open for the spectators. All the teams were in high spirits. They were dressed in their school colours. The spectators were expecting a well-contested and lively game.After the matches we went 2nd by 1-2,1-0,4-5,1-3,3-0,2-1.

Inter School Science Project Competition
Won second price in inter school Science Project competition

We hold Inter-school Science Competition. All the children can show their channel and the best one of them are kept in the wall of fame in the School. They always give enough safety for the children. We also try to keep as many project as we can in the chart or the shelves in the school. We display the modles of the competition in the shelves for everyone to see who pass by and get encouraged by their work.
